Based out of Portland Oregon, Salmon's has been making cookies since the early 2016. We here at Salmon's feel the putting our customers and our ingredients first makes the most sense. When Steve "KING" Salmon started this company he never imagined that it would grow in to the sprawling empire it is now. Customers return in troves over several different seasons.

Summer is our most popular season and this year we are happy to be presenting several new flavors and product types. Our Organic product line is proudly featuring several new fruits including Golden Apple, Watermelon, Blueberry, Strawberry, Kiwi, Banana, Wild Cherry, Grape, and many others. Not only are these cookies extremely delicious but they help save the planet.

You may very resonably be asking yourself just exactly how do these cookies save the planet? Good Question! Our first priority is to make sure our cookies are not only delicious and healthy for our customers but also for our planet. Each of the ingredients is reviewed by our team of highly trained badges to sniff out potential source of pollution. Each of our vendors is randomly audited and required to keep a detailed log of everything they sell to us. You can trust us when we say we are thorough and stand behind our products.
